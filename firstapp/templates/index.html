ABOUT Human Foosball

Number of Participants:
4 + 1 sub(optional).
Duration: 6 mins per half.
Format : Knockout

Separate Knockout rounds for girls and boys.


Foosball Team Formation : 1 GoalKeeper
and 3 Outfield Players.

Entry Fee : ₹ 200
Draw decider: Penalties(3 takes),
From one end to another and open goal.

Match Rules:
Foosbal is a table-top game that is loosely based on football.

Human foosball is the same thing as regular foosball, but life-sized! with rods stretching across the field. The players hold on to the rods in set positions.

Play starts with center start and players are not supposed to lift their hands off the rods.
And if the ball goes out of field the opponent team gets the possession. Players are not allowed to change positions during the play.
Best Team Wins (Highest scoring Team).
Warnings
Warnings will be given if they commit a foul( lifting hand off the rods /Intentionally hurting the opponent ),
For the second warning they’ll be shown a yellow card and they’ll have to sit out for 1 min , On third warning
they’ll be shown a red card and they can’t play the rest of the match.
